MotoSpeedo – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-07-19

Overview

MotoSpeedo is a GPS speedometer app for motorcycle riders developed by Blackadder Systems. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Location data: MotoSpeedo uses your device's GPS to calculate speed, heading, altitude, and trip distance. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Ride history: Completed trip summaries (distance, duration, max speed, average speed) are stored locally on your device in an on-device database. They are not uploaded or shared.

Settings: Your preferences (units, speed warning threshold, night mode, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not collect or transmit your GPS coordinates to any server.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Permissions

Location (Fine & Coarse): Required to provide speedometer functionality. Used only while the app is actively running.

Foreground Service (Location): Allows the speedometer to continue tracking during an active trip when the screen is off or another app is in the foreground.

Notifications: Used to display a persistent notification while the location tracking service is running, as required by Android.

Data Retention

Ride history records are automatically deleted after 30 days. You can also manually delete individual rides or clear all history from within the app.

Children's Privacy

MotoSpeedo is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the app's GitHub repository or contact Blackadder Systems through the Google Play developer page.
