MotoSpeedo – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-16

Overview

MotoSpeedo is a simple GPS speedometer app for motorcycle riders, developed by Blackadder Systems. We do not collect any data — ever, for any reason.

No Data Collection

MotoSpeedo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no network requests of any kind.

Your GPS location is used solely to calculate your speed, heading, and altitude on your device in real time. It is never recorded, uploaded, or shared.

Trip summaries (distance, duration, speed) are saved locally on your device only and are never transmitted anywhere. You can delete them at any time from within the app.

Permissions

Location: Required to provide speedometer functionality. Used only while the app is running on your device.

Foreground Service: Allows the speedometer to keep running while your screen is on during a ride, as required by Android.

Notifications: Used to show a status notification while the GPS service is active, as required by Android for foreground services.

Contact

Questions? Contact Blackadder Systems via the Google Play developer page.
